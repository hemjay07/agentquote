## Red Flags & Course Correction

If you see these issues, here's how to respond:

### "No traffic after 4 weeks"
- **Check**: Are pages indexed in GSC? (Go to Coverage report)
- **Action**: Submit sitemap, fix indexing errors
- **Timeline**: Usually 1-2 weeks to show in search results

### "High bounce rate on blog posts (>70%)"
- **Check**: Is content matching intent? Are headlines clear?
- **Action**: Rewrite intro, add table of contents, improve formatting
- **Timeline**: Update existing posts, resubmit to GSC

### "Keywords not moving after 8 weeks"
- **Check**: Are we in top 100 at all? Compare to top-ranking pages
- **Action**: Improve content quality (longer, better examples), earn backlinks
- **Timeline**: 4-8 weeks to see movement

### "No backlinks after 8 weeks"
- **Check**: Are outreach emails personalized? Is pitch clear?
- **Action**: Make subject lines more specific, personalize each pitch, target higher-authority sites
- **Timeline**: 2-3 week email-to-response time typical
